### Recovery Protocol

Recovery Triggers:

- HRV significantly below baseline → reduce intensity or convert quality to aerobic.
- Resting HR elevated meaningfully above baseline → monitor and consider modification.
- Persistent soreness, mechanical pain, or gait change → reduce run load immediately.
- Two or more red flags → modify or skip intensity before cutting easy aerobic volume.
- No current injuries reported; maintain proactive calf/soleus/foot/hip resilience work.

Feel Scale:

| Score | Meaning |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Excellent; fully recovered |
| 2 | Good; normal fatigue |
| 3 | Moderate; manageable tiredness |
| 4 | Fatigued; reduced readiness, modification likely |
| 5 | Exhausted; rest or very light recovery only |
